<b><h3>Fishing has never been more important- or dangerous.</h3></b>
Set a century after an apocalyptic flood, survivors cling to islands that were once mountains. Here you, a fisherman, are the difference between life and death for a town. Catch fish, swing across fjords, and wrangle fierce sea monsters- all using your fishing rod.

<b>Fangst is a hand-drawn action/adventure platformer where you catch fish and fight monsters to ensure the survival of your hometown.</b> Explore atmospheric fjords, cook tasty meals to get stronger, and get to know the strange inhabitants of this new world. Among them, two bachelors are vying for your attention. Will you reality check a bratty botanist? Or satisfy the violent cravings of a monstrous salmon warrior.<br/>

Take on a quest to unearth the truth of the flood, and learn what really happened one hundred years ago through a story-rich singleplayer experience- featuring a narrative that questions our connection to the sea, man-made environmental destruction, and what it truly means to be a man. <br/>

This is the first major title from Norwegian queer-owned studio Vertebrae Entertainment, who are excited to put a LGBTQ+ twist on the classic tale of strong men and the sea.<br/><br/>

<b><h3>FISH</h3></b>
<ul>
    <li>Customize your fishing rod</li>
    <li>Catch over 70 kinds of fish</li>
    <li>Sell your catch, or make powerful food-based buffs</li>
</ul>


Hunt real-world northern atlantic catch like salmon and cod by learning their silhouettes, preferences and locations. Some fish are a lot rarer than others, and can be sold for a golden price tag to upgrade gear- or cooked for a nice buff when facing a powerful monster! <br/>

<b><h3>FIGHT</h3></b>
<ul>
    <li>Beat one monster with another monster</li>
    <li>10 massive sea serpents to fight</li>
    <li>Harvest monster parts for upgrades</li>
</ul>


Fluid combat movement that allows you to seamlessly switch between beating the snot out of monster fish, and picking up said monster fish to beat the snot out of another monster fish. In the end, launch monsters for explosive environmental effects- and use them to your advantage. Some bigger beasts may be too tough for a fishing pole alone.<br/>

<b><h3>EXPLORE</h3></b>
<ul>
    <li>Five dangerous islands to run, swing and climb across</li>
    <li>Collect ingredients and secrets</li>
    <li>Follow leads to learn the truth of the world</li>
</ul>


The natural scenery of Galgefjord is only a facade. Explore the barely hidden remains of our civilization while collecting ingredients and quest items to rebuild your home. The key to our end is closer than you’d believe.<br/>

<b><h3>A DIFFERENT POST-APOCALYPSE</h3></b>
<ul>
    <li>A wild cast of strange characters</li>
    <li>Help rebuild a struggling town</li>
    <li>Two unusual bachelors</li>
</ul>


A strange affliction has come over humanity, turning people seemingly at random into fish mutants. But life goes on, and we face the same trouble as always- people need somewhere safe to sleep and food to eat. That's where you come in!<br/><br/>
